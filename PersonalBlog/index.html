RFID-EDACAMS

The application of RFID to door access control and student attendance monitoring is capable of recording time, knowing the person who accessed a certain room and eliminating time wasted during manual collection of attendance and an opportunity for the educational administrators to capture face-to-face classroom statistics for allocation of appropriate attendance scores and for further managerial decisions.

Radio Frequency Identification Enabled Door Access Control and Management System (RFID-EDACAMS) was made for my thesis project with my partner. On the hardware part, we used Raspberry Pi 3, NFC Reader, LCD Touchscreen, Magnetic Door Lock, Push Button Door Exit, Relay, Router, Micro SD Card and Power Supply for all the components while on the software part, we used MariaDB for the storing of datas and Qt for creating classic and embedded Graphical User Interfaces (GUI) and application using c++ language. In order to test our project, we created a simple enrollment system for us to get their unique RFIDs together with their personal information.

  • Date: March 2018
  • Database Management System: MariaDB
  • Programming Language: c++
